The merge function¶

import pandas as pd

left_df = pd.DataFrame({
                    'firm': ['Accenture','Citi','GS'],
                    'varA': ['A1', 'A2', 'A3']})

right_df = pd.DataFrame({
                    'firm': ['GS','Chase','WF'],
                    'varB': ['B1', 'B2', 'B3'],
                    'varc': ['C1', 'C2', 'C3']})
